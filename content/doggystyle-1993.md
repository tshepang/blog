The best hip-hop album I've ever come across, it has not one let-down
of a track. The closest competitor is *The Score* (there is only 1
blight in there). Such an amazing achievement!

1 masterpiece
=============

-   Doggy Dogg World

7 excellent tracks
==================

-   F Funk Intro
-   Gin And Juice
-   Murder Was the Case
-   Serial Killa
-   For All My Niggaz & Bitches
-   Gz & Hustlas
-   Pump Pump
